UX Design Internship

Avanade, May 2019 - July 2019

From my first day, I was involved in the UX process and in my spare time I got to explore more front-end technologies. Over the course of the summer, I worked with other interns and designers across the US. We brainstormed on paper and collaborative whiteboards. I designed a wireframe and clickable prototype in Adobe XD. Our team had daily stand-ups where I received feedback and made revisions.

Further along in the internship, I researched platforms to use for the final product. I helped create a web prototype using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

In my final week, I analyzed interview audio and transcripts. I created an affinity diagram and made a persona so our team and the client could understand our users better.
