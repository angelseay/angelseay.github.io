☆ I became interested in computer science after attending several programming summer camps in high school. This new approach to problem solving really interested me. But, at the time, I was more fascinated with the artistic side: graphic design. So when it came time for me to choose my major, I found the one that allowed me to combine my knack for problem solving with my passion for art. I knew that as a creator I wanted to use any platform I could to help others. And the methods and disciplines of Georgia Tech's computer science program and my threads, Media and People, perfectly aligned with that.
